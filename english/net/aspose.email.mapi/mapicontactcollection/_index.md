## Remarks

This collection class extends List and supports all standard list operations such as adding, removing, and iterating through contact items. It also supports implicit conversion from arrays of [`MapiContact`](../mapicontact/) objects for convenient initialization. Use this collection when working with multiple contacts, such as when exporting or importing contact data in MAPI format.
